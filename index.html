Culture Playbook Guide

The "why and how" behind each tactic, drawn from Peach's Culture document, the CCO role description, and the Level 11 culture playbook. Use this with Kelly to onboard her into the Chief Culture Officer role.

The Peach culture model: Culture → values → behaviour → change.

Culture is operationalized through three things working together: a culture document (the "why"), a Chief Culture Officer (weekly ownership — now Kelly), and a culture playbook of tactics that are tangible, simple, and inexpensive. Add one tactic at a time and let it stick before layering on the next.
